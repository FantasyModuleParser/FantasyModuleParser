Swarm of Crawling Claws
Medium swarm of tiny undead, neutral evil
Armor Class 12
Hit Points 54 (12d8)
Speed 20 ft., climb 20 ft.
STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
20 (+5) 14 (+2) 11 (+0) 5 (-3) 10 (+0) 4 (-3)
Damage Resistances bludgeoning, piercing, slashing
Damage Immunities poison
Condition Immunities charmed, exhaustion, poisoned
Senses blindsight 30 ft. (blind beyond this radius), passive
Perception 10
Languages understands Common but can't speak
Challenge 3 (700 XP)
Swarm. The swarm can occupy another creature's space and
vice versa, and the swarm can move through any opening large
enough for a claw. The swarm can't regain hit points or gain
temporary hit points.
Turn Immunity. The claw is immune to effects that turn undead.
Actions
Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit:
19 (4d6 + 5) bludgeoning or slashing damage (claw's choice), or
12 (2d6 + 5) bludgeoning or slashing damage if the swarm has half
of its hit points or fewer, and the target must succeed on a DC 15
Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.
