Aarakocra Brave
Medium humanoid (aarakocra), neutral good
Armor Class 14
Hit Points 78 (12d8 + 24)
Speed 30 ft., fly 60 ft.
STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
10 (+0) 18 (+4) 14 (+1) 11 (+0) 13 (+1) 11 (+0)
Skills Acrobatics +6, Perception +5
Senses passive Perception 15
Languages Auran
Challenge 3 (700 XP)
Dive Attack. If the aarakocra is flying and dives at least 30 feet
straight toward a target and then hits it with a melee weapon attack,
the attack deals an extra 10 (3d6) damage to the target.
Flyby. The aarakocra doesn't provoke an opportunity attack when it
flies out of an enemy's reach.
Actions
Multiattack. The aarakocra makes three melee attacks: one with its
javelin and two with its talons, or two ranged attacks.
Talon. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.
Hit: 7 (ld6 + 4) slashing damage.
Javelin. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft. or
range 30/120 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d6 + 4) piercing damage.
