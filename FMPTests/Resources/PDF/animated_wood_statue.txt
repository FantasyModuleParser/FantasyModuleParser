Antimagic Susceptibility. The statue is incapacitated while in the
area of an antimagic field. If targeted by dispel magic, the statue
must succeed on a Constitution saving throw against the caster's
spell save DC or fall unconscious for 1 minute.

indistinguishable from a normal statue.
